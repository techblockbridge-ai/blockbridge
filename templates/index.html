💡
How anomaly detection works: For each asset, BlockBridge maintains a rolling window of recent borrow amounts. When a new borrow arrives, it calculates the z-score — the number of standard deviations above the mean. A z-score of 2.0 means the borrow is larger than ~97.7% of recent borrows for that asset. The risk score (0–10) is derived from z-score magnitude, scaled between your threshold and 6σ. The Ollama LLM (running locally on imhotep) generates a plain-language narrative for each flagged event.
