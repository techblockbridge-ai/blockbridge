Health Factor
Liquidation risk assessment for this position
What is a health factor?
Think of it like a credit score for this loan. If the health factor drops below 1.0, the position gets automatically liquidated — meaning the protocol sells the borrower's collateral to cover the loan. A health factor of 1.5 means there is 50% buffer before liquidation. The CRV incident in November 2023 had a health factor approaching 1.05 before intervention.
